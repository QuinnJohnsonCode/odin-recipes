Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a simple to make protein packed meal.
This meal is perfect for families who love huge portions!

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Canned Tomatoes
  • Fresh Herbs
  • Sugar
  • Spices and Seasonings
  • Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Cook the meat
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the cheese layer
  4. Assemble the lasagna
  5. Bake the lasagna
