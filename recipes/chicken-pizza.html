Chicken Pizza

Description

This buffalo chicken pizza is sure to make a splash at next year's Superbowl party!
Mouthwatering, savoury and delicious are all words synoynmous with this recipe.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breasts
  • Hot Sauce
  • Butter
  • Blue Cheese Salad Dressing
  • Pizza Crust
  • Mozzarella

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven to 425 degrees F
  2. Mix Chicken, Hot Sauce and Butter in a bowl
  3. Place crust on baking sheet and spread salad dressing
  4. Top with chicken and sprinkle mozzarella
  5. Bake for 5 - 10 minutes
